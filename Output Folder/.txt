## Resignation Email Templates Using AI:

**Please note that I'll need some additional information to tailor the email specifically to your situation. Please tell me:**

* **Your name and the name of your manager/supervisor.**
* **The date of your last day of work.**
* **(Optional) The reason for leaving (e.g., new opportunity, personal reasons).**
* **(Optional) Whether you'd like to offer assistance with the transition.**

**Here are a few templates based on different scenarios, which you can customize further:**

**Template 1: Simple and Direct**

Subject: Resignation - [Your Name]

Dear [Manager's Name],

Please accept this email as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as [Your Position] at [Company Name]. My last day of employment will be [Your Last Day].

Thank you for the opportunity to work at [Company Name]. I've enjoyed my time here and appreciate the opportunities I've been given.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

**Template 2: Expressing Gratitude and Offering Assistance**

Subject: Resignation - [Your Name] and Transition Offer

Dear [Manager's Name],

Please accept this email as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as [Your Position] at [Company Name]. My last day of employment will be [Your Last Day].

I want to express my sincere gratitude for the opportunities for professional and personal development that I have been provided during my time at [Company Name]. I have truly enjoyed my time working here and appreciate the support and encouragement that you and the team have provided me.

To ensure a smooth transition, I am happy to assist in any way possible with training my replacement and documenting my current projects. Please let me know how I can be of help.

I wish you and the company all the best in the future. 

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

**Template 3: Sharing Reason for Leaving (New Opportunity)**

Subject: Resignation - [Your Name]

Dear [Manager's Name],

Please accept this email as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as [Your Position] at [Company Name]. My last day of employment will be [Your Last Day].

I have recently accepted a position at another company that will allow me to further my career goals and pursue new challenges. 

I am grateful for the experiences and opportunities I have gained while working at [Company Name] and I wish you and the company continued success.

Sincerely,
